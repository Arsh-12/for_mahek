My dearest Mahek,

You once asked me — do you really love me? And I realised that I'd been holding the answer inside me every single day, but never found the right words to say it out loud. So today, I am saying it clearly, with no doubt and no hesitation:

I love you. I truly, completely, wholeheartedly love you.

I love you in closeness and in distance. I love you in satisfaction and in defiance. My love for you is not a feeling that comes and goes — it is a promise. And my promise never changes.

I know I go quiet when I should speak. I know I assume you already feel what I feel. But Mahek — the fact that I don't say it enough does not mean it isn't there. You are in every thought, every plan, every version of my future.

The rest of the world can wait. You are all that matters.

Always and forever yours,

Arshad

My promises to you

🤝 I promise to never leave your side — not when things are hard, not when silence fills the room, not when the world pulls us in different directions.
🗣️ I promise to say it out loud — to stop keeping my love quiet inside my chest and to make sure you feel it every single day.
🌙 I promise to love you in every version of life — in the easy mornings and the hard nights, in joy and in the moments that test us.
💍 I promise that my love never changes. Distance, time, arguments, silence — none of it changes what I feel. You are my constant.
✨ I promise to choose you, over and over — not because I have to, but because there is no version of happiness that makes sense without you in it.
💕

Arshad loves Mahek — yesterday, today, and always.
